Welcome to the Student Portal

The Student Portal is your one-stop destination for all academic resources, announcements, and updates. Here, you can easily navigate through various features tailored to enhance your learning experience.

  • Quick Links: Access important links like course schedules, grades, and academic calendars.
  • Latest Announcements: Stay updated with the latest news from the administration and faculty.
  • Access Current Subjects: Section from the main dashboard. From there, you can explore your grade or strand and utilize all available features.

Our Mission

At the Student Portal, our mission is to empower students with easy access to essential academic information and resources. We aim to foster a supportive community that promotes learning and collaboration.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of educators and technologists committed to enhancing the student experience. Our platform is designed with user-friendliness in mind, ensuring that every student can find what they need quickly and efficiently.

Offers for Senior Highschool
  • General Academic Strand (GAS): Focus on core subjects to prepare for higher education.
  • Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL): Hands-on training in various technical fields.
  • Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMMS): Specialized programs for aspiring athletes.
  • Account-Based Marketing (ABM): Creative courses for future artists and designers.
  • Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM): Emphasis on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.
  • Scholarship Opportunities: Financial aid options available for eligible students.
