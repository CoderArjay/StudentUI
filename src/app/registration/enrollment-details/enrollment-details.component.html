Enrollment Process
Personal Information: Fill up the required fields in the Personal Information form, including your full name, date of birth, gender, address, and contact information. Ensure all details are accurate to avoid issues later. Enrollment Details: Provide your enrollment details such as the grade level you are applying for, the strand (if applicable), and any previous school information. This helps us understand your academic background. Payment: Review the tuition fees and payment options available. Make sure to complete the payment process by the specified deadline to secure your enrollment. DSF Approval: Await approval from the Department of Student Affairs (DSF). You may need to provide additional documents or information if requested. Check your email regularly for updates on your application status. Student Contract: Review and sign the student contract, which outlines the terms and conditions of enrollment. Make sure you understand all clauses before signing. Official Registration: Wait for the registration decision from the school administration. Follow up as needed to ensure your application is processed in a timely manner. Once approved, you will receive confirmation and further instructions.
